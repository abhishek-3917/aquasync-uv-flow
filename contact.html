Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the UV purification process take?

The UV-C purification cycle takes just 90 seconds to eliminate 99.9% of harmful bacteria and viruses.

How often do I need to replace the filters?

Filters should be replaced every 2-3 months depending on usage. The smart app will notify you when it's time for replacement.

Is the bottle dishwasher safe?

The bottle body is dishwasher safe, but we recommend hand washing the cap and UV module to preserve the electronics.

What is the warranty period?

All Aquasync products come with a 2-year comprehensive warranty covering manufacturing defects and electronic components.
